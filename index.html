Why We're Building This

AI is everywhere. Judgment isn't.

Developers reach for AI reflexively without developing the judgment to use it well. Recruiters have no way to measure it. We're building the bridge.

🎯

Anyone can prompt. Few can build.

AI has made it trivially easy to generate code — but shipping a prototype is not the same as building software. The gap between vibe-coding and real engineering is where most developers get stuck.

🏢

Hiring teams are flying blind.

Traditional interviews ignore the AI dimension entirely. There's no reliable way to assess whether a candidate can actually leverage AI tools — or if they're just copy-pasting output they don't understand.

🚀

AI Builder Arena is the fix.

Structured, real-world challenges that teach developers how to collaborate with AI — not just use it. Every challenge builds judgment, speed, and technical excellence. Every completion earns a verifiable credential.
